பல்லாங்குழி — About Us

Preserving Tamil heritage, one shell at a time.

Who We Are

We are Quixote Club, a small independent team passionate about preserving traditional South Indian games in a modern digital format. Our mission is to make ancient Tamil culture accessible and enjoyable to everyone around the world — for free.

Our Game

Pallankuzhi (பல்லாங்குழி) is one of the oldest traditional board games of Tamil Nadu, with roots going back over 2,000 years. It is a game of sowing and capture, traditionally played on a wooden board with cowrie shells or tamarind seeds.

Our digital version brings this ancient game to life with beautiful animations, authentic sounds, and faithful gameplay — playable on any device, completely free.

Features

🐚 Animated cowrie shells · 🎨 Traditional kolam-inspired board art · 🎵 Ambient sounds · 🤖 Player vs AI · 🎮 Player vs Player · 📱 Works on mobile, tablet and desktop

Our Mission

We believe traditional games are living heritage. By bringing Pallankuzhi online, we hope to introduce this beautiful game to new generations and keep Tamil culture alive in the digital age.
