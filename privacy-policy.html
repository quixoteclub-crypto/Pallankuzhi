Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025  ·  Last Updated: May 16, 2026

Short version: Pallankuzhi does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The game runs entirely on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no ads.

1. Who We Are

Pallankuzhi is a digital board game developed and maintained by Quixote Club ("we", "us", or "our"). You can contact us at quixoteclub247@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). Specifically:

  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or location.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or crash-reporting services.
  • We do not track your gameplay behavior or device identifiers.

3. Local Storage

The app may store minimal data locally on your device (such as your current game state or score history) using your browser's or device's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

The web version of this app loads the following third-party libraries via CDN solely to power the game's interface:

  • React 18 — cdnjs.cloudflare.com
  • Babel Standalone — cdnjs.cloudflare.com
  • Google Fonts (Cinzel, Crimson Text) — fonts.google.com

These services may log your IP address as part of standard CDN/DNS operation, governed by their own privacy policies. We have no access to those logs.

5. Children's Privacy

Pallankuzhi is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or under 16 in regions where that threshold applies). Since we collect no data at all, there is nothing to delete or request access to.

6. Your Rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is no data to access, rectify, export, or delete. If you have questions about this, please contact us at quixoteclub247@gmail.com.

7. Security

The app runs entirely client-side. No user data is transmitted to our servers. The game board state and scores exist only in your device's memory during your session.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this policy (for example, if future versions add optional features), we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and notify users through the app store listing.

9. Contact
