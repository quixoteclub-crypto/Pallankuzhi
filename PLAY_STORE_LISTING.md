## Full Description (4000 chars max)

```
பல்லாங்குழி (Pallankuzhi) is one of India's oldest and most beloved board games,
played for thousands of years across Tamil Nadu and South Asia. Now you can carry
this ancient tradition in your pocket.

🐚 AUTHENTIC GAMEPLAY
Sow cowrie shells across the wooden board, capture your opponent's pits, and
claim seeds from forbidden pits at game's end. The rules follow the traditional
7×2 format faithfully — forbidden pits, relay moves, and all.

🤖 SMART AI OPPONENT
Challenge a capable AI that picks optimal moves. Perfect for learning the game
or sharpening your strategy before playing with family.

✨ BEAUTIFUL PRESENTATION
• Handcrafted wooden board aesthetic with carved Tamil motifs
• Animated cowrie shells that fly, bounce, and settle into pits
• Expressive hand animation shows every pick-up and sow
• Authentic sound design — shell clacks, captures, and victory fanfares
• Fully playable offline — no internet required after first load

🎮 HOW TO PLAY
Each player controls 7 pits on their side. On your turn, pick a pit and sow its
shells one-by-one into subsequent pits going clockwise. If your last shell lands
before an occupied pit, you relay and keep sowing. If it lands before an empty
pit followed by a filled one, you capture those seeds. Forbidden pits (marked
with a special symbol) can't be picked — but the player who last visited them
at game's end claims their shells. The player with the most shells wins!

📱 FEATURES
• Single-player vs AI
• Animated seed-sowing with flying cowrie shells
• Sound effects for every action
• Accessible: screen-reader announcements, reduced-motion support
• Works offline (PWA / installable)
• No ads · No accounts · No data collected

A love letter to Tamil heritage — crafted with care.
```
